Why Scholarships Matter

Nalanda believes that finances should never be a barrier to education. We offer a range of need-based and merit-based scholarships to help aspiring students access high-quality learning resources and mentorship across disciplines and exam pathways.

Scholarships Available

Merit Excellence Award

For top 5% performers in internal assessments & mock tests. Up to 100% fee waiver available.

Need-Based Aid

Partial to full fee waivers for students from low-income backgrounds after application & review.

Girl Scholar Grant

To support female aspirants in competitive prep programs like JEE, NEET & UPSC.

Language Learning Support

Subsidized courses for regional & foreign language programs for inclusive learners.

Eligibility & Application

Eligibility varies by scholarship type. Most require either academic merit, economic need documentation, or recommendation letters. Students may be invited for interviews or online evaluations as part of the selection process.
