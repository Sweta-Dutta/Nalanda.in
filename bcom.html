Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Build a strong foundation in business, finance, and economics

Nalanda’s B.Com program blends conceptual knowledge with real-world application in finance, accounting, economics, and business strategy. It prepares you for careers in corporate finance, taxation, banking, auditing, and entrepreneurial ventures.

Program Highlights

  • 3-year UGC-recognized degree
  • Updated syllabus aligned with industry standards
  • Certifications in Tally, Excel, and Financial Modeling
  • Audit & tax training with case studies and simulations
  • Internship & placement support in top firms

Core Subjects

Financial Accounting

Ledgers, trial balance, journal entries, and balance sheet preparation.

Corporate Law

Understanding of business regulations, legal structures, and compliance.

Business Economics

Micro and macroeconomic principles tailored for commerce students.

Cost & Management Accounting

Costing techniques, budgeting, margin analysis, and managerial decisions.

Income Tax

Tax planning, returns filing, exemptions, and deductions.

Banking & Insurance

Indian banking system, risk management, credit policies, and insurance law.

Financial Management

Capital budgeting, working capital, dividend decisions, and valuation.

E-Commerce & Digital Business

Introduction to e-commerce platforms, digital payments, and cyber law.
