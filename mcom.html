Master of Commerce (M.Com)

Master the mechanics of business, finance, and strategy

The M.Com program at Nalanda equips students with analytical and strategic skills in finance, taxation, accounting, and corporate governance. This postgraduate program is ideal for commerce graduates who aspire to hold senior positions in corporate, academic, or public-sector settings.

Program Highlights

  • 2-year UGC-recognized postgraduate degree
  • Electives in Forensic Accounting, International Business, and Fintech
  • Training in industry software: Tally, SAP, Excel Modeling
  • Research opportunities in accounting and economics
  • Internships and placement guidance

Core Subjects

Advanced Accounting

Consolidation, corporate restructuring, and international standards.

Corporate Governance

Ethical leadership, stakeholder theory, and boardroom practices.

Financial Management

Capital budgeting, valuation, risk-return models, and working capital.

Research Methodology

Qualitative and quantitative research design, surveys, and SPSS.

Banking & Insurance

Commercial banking operations, credit appraisal, and IRDAI norms.

Taxation & Auditing

Income Tax Act, GST, auditing standards, and compliance reports.
