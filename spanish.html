Overview

Spanish is one of the most widely spoken languages across the globe—and Nalanda's Spanish program empowers learners with conversational, academic, and cultural fluency. Our beginner-to-advanced courses are immersive, interactive, and globally recognized.

Course Features

A1–A2 Foundation

Basics of vocabulary, grammar, greetings, everyday verbs, and cultural etiquette.

B1–B2 Intermediate

Travel dialogue, storytelling, grammar structures, and confidence-building conversation.

DELE Exam Preparation

Official prep for Instituto Cervantes certifications with task-based practice and mock sessions.

Latin Culture Modules

Explore Spanish through music, cinema, food, and folklore from Spain and Latin America.
