Overview

Nalanda’s defence entrance coaching prepares aspiring candidates for NDA (National Defence Academy), CDS (Combined Defence Services), and AFCAT (Air Force Common Admission Test) with focused modules, mock tests, and leadership mentoring. Whether you're preparing for a written test or SSB interview, our program is designed to sharpen your mind and strengthen your resolve.

What We Offer

Written Test Preparation

Comprehensive classes in Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge covering current affairs, history, and geography.

SSB Interview Guidance

Psychology tests, group tasks, personal interviews, and officer-like quality (OLQ) development support.

Mock Exams & Time Practice

Full-length papers, timed MCQs, and negative-marking strategies for NDA/CDS/AFCAT pattern alignment.

Physical Fitness Mentoring

Fitness tips and preparation advice to meet basic physical benchmarks expected by the armed forces.
