Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)

Build a scientific mindset with hands-on learning and research focus

Our B.Sc program empowers students with critical thinking, analytical skills, and solid foundations in scientific disciplines. Designed to match industry standards and academic excellence, the course prepares graduates for research, postgraduate study, or science-based careers.

Program Highlights

  • 3-year undergraduate degree recognized by UGC
  • Experienced faculty and modern lab infrastructure
  • Electives in Data Science, AI, and Biotechnology
  • Research-oriented projects and internships
  • Soft skills & career guidance workshops

Major Specializations

Physics

Mechanics, quantum physics, thermodynamics, electronics, and nuclear science.

Chemistry

Inorganic, organic, and physical chemistry, instrumentation, and green chemistry.

Biology

Genetics, molecular biology, botany, microbiology, and human physiology.

Mathematics

Calculus, linear algebra, number theory, probability, and differential equations.

Computer Science

Programming, data structures, web development, Python, and databases.

Environmental Science

Ecology, sustainability, climate change, and environmental policy.
