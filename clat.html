About CLAT

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is the national-level entrance exam for admission to prestigious National Law Universities (NLUs) across India. It evaluates proficiency in English, General Knowledge, Logical Reasoning, Legal Aptitude, and Quantitative Techniques.
