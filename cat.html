About CAT

The Common Admission Test (CAT) is India’s top-level MBA entrance exam for IIMs and other leading business schools. It tests candidates on Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Data Interpretation—challenging your speed, strategy, and smarts.
