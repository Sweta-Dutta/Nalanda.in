The BBA program at Nalanda is crafted for future business leaders, entrepreneurs, and professionals. You’ll explore management theory, real-world case studies, and strategic thinking to prepare for today's dynamic global economy.

  • Internship & placement support with top firms
  • Live business simulations & capstone projects
