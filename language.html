Language Learning

Explore new cultures, connect globally, and communicate with confidence.

Nalanda’s Language Learning courses are designed to help learners of all ages master new languages and embrace global fluency. Whether you're learning for travel, academics, business, or personal growth, we offer beginner to advanced tracks across popular global and regional languages.

Program Highlights

  • Self-paced, instructor-led, and hybrid options
  • CEFR-aligned curriculum (A1 to C2 levels)
  • Live conversation practice with native speakers
  • Cultural immersion videos, music, and literature
  • Final certificate with proficiency level & course hours
