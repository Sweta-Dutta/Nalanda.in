Overview

Nalanda’s Chinese (Mandarin) courses are structured around practical communication, cultural understanding, and script literacy. Our beginner-to-advanced tracks prepare learners to listen, speak, read, and write with confidence using Pinyin and simplified characters.

Program Features

Beginner Modules

Pinyin, tones, greetings, numbers, days of the week, and sentence basics.

Hanzi Literacy

Stroke order, radicals, character recognition, and word formation practices.

HSK Exam Prep

Mandarin proficiency certification prep from HSK 1 to HSK 5 with mock tests and audio drills.

Chinese Culture Lab

Idioms, calligraphy, traditions, and conversational etiquette in real-life context.
