Overview

Our French courses immerse learners in the language of diplomacy, fashion, and global culture. Whether you’re a total beginner or preparing for the DELF exam, Nalanda’s structured modules help you build listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills in real-world contexts.

Course Highlights

A1–A2 Beginner Levels

Alphabet, introductions, everyday expressions, shopping and city vocabulary.

B1–B2 Intermediate

Conversation practice, grammar tenses, opinion writing, and cultural insights.

DELF/DALF Prep

Mock tests, comprehension drills, production tasks, and oral simulations.

Francophone Culture

Music, cinema, cuisine, and idiomatic expressions that bring French to life.
