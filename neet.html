About NEET

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is a national-level entrance exam for MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, and other undergraduate medical programs in India. NEET assesses knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (Botany & Zoology).
